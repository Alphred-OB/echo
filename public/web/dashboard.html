Security status

Why this account has no password to steal.

One-time sound codes active
Every sign-in plays a unique random code — single-use, 30-second expiry. Recording it is useless.
Device-bound keys active
Private keys are generated on your phone and physically cannot be copied off it.
User consent active
Your phone never signs silently — every sign-in shows an approve / deny prompt.
Rate-limited recovery active
Recovery codes are stored only as hashes; five failed attempts locks recovery for 15 minutes.
Relay attacks known limit
An attacker streaming live audio to a remote phone is mitigated by consent prompts and short range, not yet by distance bounding.
